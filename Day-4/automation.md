# Salesforce Automation – Precise Notes

## Why Automation Matters
Automation helps businesses perform tasks automatically without manual effort.

### Benefits
* Saves time
* Reduces human errors
* Improves productivity
* Ensures faster processes
* Enhances customer experience
---

# Different Automation Types
## 1. Flow Automation
Salesforce Flow Builder used to automate business processes with low/no code.

## 2. Approval Automation
Automatically sends records for approval.

## 3. Email Automation
Sends automatic emails and notifications.

## 4. Scheduled Automation
Runs tasks automatically at a specific time.
---

# Business Process Improvement
Automation improves business operations by:
* Reducing repetitive work
* Increasing efficiency
* Improving data accuracy
* Speeding up decision-making
* Helping teams focus on important tasks
