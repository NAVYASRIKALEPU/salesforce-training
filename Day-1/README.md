# Day 1 - Salesforce CRM Basics

## What is CRM?
CRM helps businesses manage customer relationships.
CRM (Customer Relationship Management) is a system used to manage customer data, communication, sales, marketing, and support in one place.

Salesforce Customer 360 is a CRM platform that helps companies connect all customer information and departments together.

Main Idea

CRM helps businesses:

Understand customers better
Improve customer relationships
Manage sales and support efficiently

## What is Salesforce Customer 360?
Salesforce Customer 360 connects teams, data, AI, and automation on one platform.
Companies use Salesforce because it:

Connects all teams on one platform
Provides real-time customer data
Uses AI and automation
Improves productivity
Reduces IT and support costs
Increases sales and revenue

Important Benefits:
26% increase in employee productivity
29% increase in sales productivity
Up to 20% lower annual spending

Real Business Problems Solved Using Salesforce
Problem 1: Disconnected Systems
Different departments use separate apps and spreadsheets.
Solution:
Salesforce connects everything in one platform.

Problem 2: Slow Manual Work
Employees waste time entering data and updating spreadsheets.
Solution:
Automation handles repetitive tasks automatically.

Problem 3: Poor Communication Between Teams
Marketing, sales, and support teams cannot share information properly.
Solution:
Salesforce enables seamless hand-offs and shared customer data.

Problem 4: High IT and Support Costs
Managing many systems becomes expensive.
Solution:
Salesforce reduces complexity and automates workflows.

Problem 5: Poor Customer Experience
Customers face delays and inconsistent support.
Solution
AI, chatbots, and omni-channel support improve customer service.
