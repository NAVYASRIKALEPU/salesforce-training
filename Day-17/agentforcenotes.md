# Agentforce Notes

## What Agentforce Is

Agentforce is Salesforce's platform for building and deploying autonomous AI agents that can perform tasks, answer questions, and take actions using business data and workflows.

### Key Features

* Natural language interactions.
* Automation of business processes.
* Integration with Salesforce data and applications.
* Ability to perform actions based on user requests.
* Support for customer, employee, sales, and service use cases.

### Benefits

* Increased productivity.
* Faster response times.
* Reduced manual work.
* Improved customer experience.

---

## AI Agents in Enterprise Systems

AI agents are intelligent software systems that can understand requests, make decisions, and execute tasks with minimal human intervention.

### Capabilities

* Answer customer questions.
* Retrieve and analyze data.
* Automate routine tasks.
* Generate recommendations.
* Trigger workflows and business processes.

### Enterprise Benefits

* 24/7 availability.
* Consistent service delivery.
* Scalable operations.
* Improved efficiency and accuracy.
